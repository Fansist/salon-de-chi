About the Salon

Chi’s craft, since 1996

Salon de Chi is a boutique one-stylist salon inside Phenix Salon Suites, run by Chiharu (“Chi”), a Japanese stylist whose professional career began in Japan in 1996. The salon was established in San Diego in 2016.

Chi specializes in Japanese hair straightening (thermal reconditioning), coloring, and precision cuts using the Toni & Guy scissor-cutting method, tailored to curly, fine, and thick straight hair.

The studio keeps things intimate: one stylist, one chair, and undivided attention for every appointment.
